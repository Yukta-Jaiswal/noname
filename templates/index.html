Why Choose Mentor?

You may be a rising star and have a track record of success. But when starting a new job, having a mentor to show you the ropes is important. Mentors hold a very valuable key to your success; they’ve been where you want to go. Why try to reinvent the wheel when you can learn from a trusted advisor? Your mentor can tell you everything you need to know, including those things you wouldn’t necessarily find in the official “user’s manual.” Mentor provides a platform for those students who need a insight for courses they will take in for the semester.It will also be a platform for those who believe that and are always ready to provide a helping hand.
